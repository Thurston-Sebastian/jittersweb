What is Jitters?

Jitters is a local coffee shop located in Lexington, Tennessee. Although it is also the only official coffee shop in Lexington, Tennessee; Jitters has a variety of things to provide to its customers with its extensive menu. Jitters is also a great place for the early bird as it is open from Monday - Saturday, 6AM to 4PM.
